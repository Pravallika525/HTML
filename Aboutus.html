The Medarametla Anjamma Mastan Rao Memorial Service Society was established in the year 2004 with registration no. 686/2004 under the chairmanship of Mr. Medarametla Rama Seshagiri Rao. Nearly 4000 students are studying, and 250 employee families are travelling with this society. The society boasts adequate infrastructure and institutional facilities, providing educational services irrespective of caste, creed, or religion. The Society holds around 1 Crore Fixed Deposits in Nationalized banks and 17.78 acres of land in Kesanupalli Village of Narasaraopet Mandal. The turnover of this society is almost 5 Crores per year. About Members of the Society M.R. Seshagiri Rao M.R. Seshagiri Rao, M.Sc.Ed., M.A.(Ed.), M.Phil., Chairman of this society, is an eminent person in the education field with more than 25 years of experience. He strongly believes that solving unemployment in the country can be achieved by providing technical skills to the youth. This strong concept motivated him to start this society and work toward his cherished goal. He was honored with the EXCELLENCE SERVICE AWARD for education in 2017 by the Honorable Vice Chancellor Dr. Rajendra Prasad, Acharya Nagarjuna University, for his service to the student community.
